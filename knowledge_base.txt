Welcome to Panchkula Tech Hub.

About Us:
Panchkula Tech Hub is a premier co-working space and technology incubator. We provide workspace solutions, networking events, and mentorship programs for startups, remote workers, and freelancers.

Location and Hours:
We are located at Sector 20, Panchkula, Haryana, India.
Our building is open to active members 24/7 via keycard access.
The reception desk and administrative staff are available Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST.

Membership Tiers:
1. Hot Desk: Access to any open desk in the common area. Price: ₹5,000 per month.
2. Dedicated Desk: A reserved desk just for you, including a lockable filing cabinet. Price: ₹8,000 per month.
3. Private Office: Fully furnished enclosed offices for teams of 2 to 10 people. Prices start at ₹20,000 per month.

Amenities:
All memberships include high-speed fiber-optic Wi-Fi, unlimited roasted coffee and green tea, access to heavy-duty printing facilities, and complimentary booking of meeting rooms for up to 4 hours a week.

Meeting Rooms:
We have three meeting rooms available:
- The Alpha Room: Seats 4 people, equipped with a whiteboard.
- The Beta Room: Seats 8 people, equipped with a 4K TV and video conferencing tools.
- The Gamma Boardroom: Seats 15 people, equipped with dual screens and premium audio.

Visitor Policy:
Members can bring up to 3 guests at a time. Guests must sign in at the reception desk upon arrival. For guests staying longer than 2 hours, a day pass must be purchased for ₹500.
